Data Protection Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

We respect your privacy. This notice explains how information is gathered when you browse this website, operated by stake-lido.github.io ("we", "us", or "our"), how it may be handled, and the controls you can exercise.

About this site

This website is a free, advertising-supported information resource that makes available content and resources about its topics. It may display advertising, analytics, embedded media, or links to other online services. We do not sell products or take payments directly through this site.

Information we may collect

We may collect information that your browser transmits automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device information, the page that referred you, pages visited, time spent, and estimated location inferred from technical data.

If you contact us, we may use the information you supply, such as your name, email address, and message content.

Cookies and advertising technologies

This site uses cookies, local storage, pixels, and similar technologies to store preferences, analyze usage, enhance the site, protect against misuse, and support advertising.

Third-party vendors, including Google and Microsoft Advertising, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google and its partners use advertising cookies to serve ads informed by visits to this site and elsewhere.

You can opt out of personalised advertising from Google by visiting Google Ads Settings . Where ads are served through Microsoft, you can review the Microsoft Privacy Statement and opt out at Microsoft ad settings . You can also opt out of other vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at www.aboutads.info (US) or www.youronlinechoices.eu (EEA/UK). You can adjust or block cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work if cookies are disabled.

Where required by law, advertising and analytics cookies are used only with your consent, which may be requested through a consent tool where one is shown; you can also manage or withdraw your choices through your browser settings and the opt-out links above.

How we use information

Information may be processed to run the website, protect against fraud and abuse, learn what visitors find useful, improve pages, respond to inquiries, comply with legal obligations, and support advertising or analytics.

Where required by law, including the EU/UK GDPR, we process personal data on the legal bases of your consent (for advertising and analytics cookies), our legitimate interests (in operating, securing, and improving the site), and compliance with legal obligations.

Third-party services

We may use hosting providers, analytics providers, advertising networks, security tools, and other service providers. These third parties may process data under their own privacy policies.

Besides Google, other third-party vendors and ad networks may display ads here. A current list of Google's ad technology providers is available on Google's ad technology providers page , and you may opt out of personalised advertising at www.aboutads.info . For how Google uses information from sites that use its services, see Google's notice on how it uses data from partner sites .

External websites linked from this site are not operated by us. Review their privacy policies before providing information to them.

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland

If you visit from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, personalised advertising cookies are used only with your consent, which may be requested through a consent tool where one is shown. The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you can withdraw at any time, and you can manage cookies through your browser settings. If you do not consent, you may still see non-personalised ads.

Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as permitted by law. Server logs and analytics data are typically retained for a short period and then deleted or anonymised.

Our service providers may store data in countries outside your own, including the United States. Where required, such transfers are made under appropriate safeguards, such as the Standard Contractual Clauses or the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

You can exercise the advertising opt-out using the cookie and ad controls described above or at www.aboutads.info , and we do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

How we protect data

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to safeguard information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Protecting children

This website is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where local law sets a higher age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us at admin@stake-lido.github.io and we will delete it.
